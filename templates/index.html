OrganoStation

Recordings in 3D!

We engineered a miniaturized customized system to obtain electroencephalogram (EEG) signals in brain organoids or other in vitro preparations. The system comprises 128 micro-electrodes allocated in less than one square millimeter permitting high resolutive invasive measures.

Technical specifications

  • Acquistion system
  • Micromanipulation
  • Wide-field microscopy
  • Drug application system

Electronics and acquisition system

  • Multiplexer board for the acquistion of up to 512 channels at 30 kHz via USB 3.0.
  • Low-noise recording headstage microchips for signal amplification.
  • Serial peripheral interface cables connecting board to headstage.
  • Sharpened extracellular probes with customized number and location of electrodes.

Electrical isolation and micromanipulation

  • Precise (< 1 μm) motorized (4.8 nm resolution) XYZ micromanipulators with 25 mm travel distance.
  • XYZ manual controlled (25 mm) stage oxidized to reduce toxicity or chemical degradation.
  • Vibration-damping optical table (600 x 900 mm L x W) and the associated passive-isolation table.
  • Electromagnetic isolator (< 55 dB) with computer ergonomic solution (600 x 900 x 600, L x W x H).

Wide-field fluorescence imaging

  • Large (20-80 mm) working distance and field of view (12 x 9 mm) with 0.6-1.7X magnification range.
  • Computer vision infrared video-camera with 3.5 um per pixel resolution up to 227 frames per second.
  • Broadband or single-colored (e.g., 470 nm) strong LED illumination (up to 1200 mW).
  • Filter sets for green (470 BP/495 LP/525 BP) or red (590 BP/660 LP/700 BP) fluorophores.

Drug application system

  • Dual-channel temperature controller for solution heating and sample temperature preservation.
  • Peristaltic application of small volumes (10-25 ml) under controlled recirculation (0-30 ml/min).
  • Temperature-controlled quick-exchange recording chamber (9-13 mm diameter).
  • PVC-based adaptations for tissue oxygenation and drug exchange.
